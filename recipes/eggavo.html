Avocado Toast with Egg

Description

This is a simple and delicious option for breakfast, which just involves spreading some avocado on toast and adding an egg over the top to complete the meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of Bread
  • 1 medium sized Avocado
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Hot or Siracha sauce

Steps

  1. Take your pieces of bread and toast using your preffered method (toaster or over the stove with some butter).
  2. While the bread is being toasted, take your avocado and slice it vertically through the center, separating into two tall pieces.
  3. To each piece of the avocado, take a knife and cut into it like a grid so as to make multiple cubes of avocado.
  4. Using a spoon, scoop and spread each avocado half on top of each piece of toast. Add salt and black pepper to taste.
  5. Using a medium-sized pan with some oil coating the bottom over low-medium heat, crack your eggs in. Cover the pan and allow the eggs to cook about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Once your eggs are a solid white, take off the heat and place eggs on your toast.
  7. Drizzle with chosen hot or siracha sauce over the top and enjoy!
