Red Enchilada Sauce

Description

This is a recipe to guide you in cooking a delicious sauce to use for an enchilada dinner.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium guajillo chiles
  • 2 medium cloves garlic
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can fire roasted diced tomatoes
  • Kosher salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Place guajillo chiles on a baking sheet and heat in oven until puffed up and aromatic, about 3-5 minutes.
  2. Place chiles, garlic, tomatoes, and salt into a blender. Puree until as smooth as possible.
  3. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat until shimmering. Press sauce through a fine-mesh strainer into saucepan. Cook sauce until thickened to consistency of tomato paste, about 5 to 7 minutes.
  4. Pour in chicken broth, bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occassionally.
  5. Taste sauce and season with salt to your taste, and finally enjoy on some enchiladas!
