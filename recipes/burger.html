Burger

Description

There is no better time than now to experience making burgers at home for the first time. This recipe will teach you all you need to know to quickly create some delicious burgers.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 lb of 80-20 ground beef
  • 2 slices of hamburger buns
  • Slice of American cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. First, take the ground beef and form it into a round patty shape.
  2. Then, place your patty in a pan over medium-high heat. Cook on each side for about 3-5 minutes.
  3. Once the patty has been cooked through, place a slice of american cheese as the patty cools down. Add salt and black pepper to taste.
  4. Prepare the bread buns with preferred condiments. Make sure to begin with the lettuce as the first layer.
  5. Place the patties over the lettuce, and layer with the rest of the veggies.
  6. Finally, the second burger bun is placed on top of the stack and served.
